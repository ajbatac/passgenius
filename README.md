## 🔥 Key Features

- **🔐 Absolute Local Security:** All processing happens entirely on your device. Zero cloud, zero databases, zero logs.
- **🧠 AI-Powered suggestions:** Intelligently configure your password constraints for specific categories like Banking, Social Media, and more.
- **🔖 Sidebar Bookmarklet:** Generate and manage passwords directly from your favorite websites with a persistent, secure side window.
- **🎨 Premium Cyber UI:** A stunning, high-contrast interface designed for maximum efficiency and aesthetic appeal.
- **🌗 Dark Mode Excellence:** Fully optimized for low-light environments with a sleek glassmorphism design.

---

## 🔖 Bookmarklet Sidebar
**Generate passwords without leaving your page.**
To use, drag the "PassGenius Sidebar" button from the app directly to your browser's bookmarks bar. Clicking it opens a persistent sidebar for instant password orchestration.
